At Voice of Women Ambassador (VOWA), we are dedicated to providing education, training, and support to empower women and girls to reach their full potential. Our initiatives focus on promoting women's leadership, enhancing economic opportunities, and advocating for gender equality. Voice of Women Ambassador (VOWA) is a registered non-profit charity organization based in Canada and Nigeria. Our primary focus is on the safety, training, and empowerment of women, particularly in the areas of education, health, and rights awareness.
Our mission is to empower women and girls by providing education, training, and advocacy programs that equip them with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to overcome societal and cultural barriers. We aim to address issues such as gender inequality, violence, and lack of access to education, ensuring that every woman and girl has the opportunity to thrive, contribute to their communities, and lead meaningful, successful lives.
We envision a world where every woman and girl is empowered to realize her full potential, living in a society that values her contributions and provides equal opportunities for growth and leadership. Our vision is a future where gender equality is the norm, where women and girls are free from violence, discrimination, and poverty, and where they have the resources and support needed to shape their own destinies and build better, stronger communities.
